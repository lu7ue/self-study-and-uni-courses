You can check out more Info from here.

 How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

 How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Go to the webshop and there you have to log in to your HZ account. After that, you can purchase whatever you want and you can pay via PayPal.

 Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

All necessary information about COVID-19 and related instructions can be found here.

 How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

You can book it from the self service portal. When you enter you can go to 'Make a reservation', after that 'Reserve meeting room or Project Group room' and 'Reserve a room' and then you can choose a room.

 What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You need to reveal your HZ pass at the barriers. After that, you can park in the lot.

 What study programs do HZ Campuses have?

HZ has two campuses in the neighbouring towns Vlissingen and Middelburg.
Vlissingen campus: Economics Management, Social Studies, Logistics Engineering, Chemistry, and Engineering.
Middelburg campus: Building Construction, Information and Communication Technology, Industrial Engineering and Management, Delta Academy, Water Management, and Civil Engineering.

 Who are your study career coaches for the first year?

Frans Blauw, Daphne Heertjes, Elio Tolhoek, Daan de Waard.

 What are the Laptop requirements for your ICT study?

CPU with at least VT-x/AMD-v support, the internal memory (RAM) is 16GB, a battery that provides power for at least three hours, and with SSD(minimum of 250GB) instead of HDD.

 How to put money on your HZ student card?

You can find a PinPoint in the central hall (close to the reception desk). Here you can put money on your HZ student card with your bank card or Credit card. If you canâ€™t manage to put money on your HZ student card because PinPoint does not accept your bank card you can go to the HZ Financial department (Office B1.14) to put money on your HZ student card.

 How about the extracurricular language courses?

For international students, the HZ language center offers basic-level Dutch courses. These courses are not mandatory to complete the HBO-ICT program but offer a nice addition to the program for students who are interested in learning the local language. Next to this, these courses offer a total of 5EC in one year, which can be used to fill in your Personality course. You can find the details of the course and schedule here.

 How to register with the municipality?

If you are staying in the Netherlands for more than four months, you will need to go to the municipality and register as a new resident of the town where you are living. For more information please check the official website of Vlissingen or Middelburg.
